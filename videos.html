Frame-by-Frame Video Player

This page renders video by taking each decoded frame and painting it to the canvas in real time. The video element stays hidden; the canvas is the display.

Library

Videos are loaded from /videos-for-switch and optionally a Google Drive folder. Drive videos are fetched into RAM before playback. Image sequences are loaded from /frames-manifest.json and can point into any folder (including bdns-coredns, CaptiveDNS, dns-landing, and LaneChange).
